Long Time No See!

A lot has happened since my last post that made me completely forget the blog. My family got bigger with a girlfriend and a rescued greyhound, we moved 3 more times until she landed a nice job in Albacete, until this January that she got an awesome opportunity at Madrid.

Netberry

In albacete I got a job on Web & Mobile development at Netberry during the year and half that we stayed. It was a good place to work, with a nice family feeling among the co-workers.

In my first project I built a web application that works entirely offline in the browser, so a technician working on a Solar or Eolic plant without connection could still use it… I’ll write about it soon, but basically used IndexedDB for the data, Javascript the logic and Cache Manifest to let the files persist without internet.

Also improved a lot my jQuery, learned about framework internals (we used a custom PHP framework built on top of Zend), some custom scripts with PHPExcel and SEPA data format form bank domiciliations… worked on some cool and big projects, patched legacy code… but definetly got tired of PHP and it’s environment.

Whats next?

Since I’m in Madrid and there are plenty of job offers for diverse positions, I’ll finally give an opportunity for Ruby to change my career and have fun again at work. Many years has passed since I first tried, and even got a second chance to work at RoR, but life got in the way… but third time’s the charm.
